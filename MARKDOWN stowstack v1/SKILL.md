When performing facility audits for StowStack:

1. Always read context/diagnostic-framework.md before classifying problems
2. Always read context/voice-and-style.md before writing
3. Use conservative estimates for revenue calculations ($100-150 avg rent unless data says otherwise)
4. Classify every facility into at least one diagnostic bucket (A-D)
5. Be honest about Bucket D situations — don't recommend ads for problems ads can't fix
6. Every recommendation must tie to a specific occupancy or revenue impact
7. Check for the facility's Google Business profile, website, and Meta Ad Library presence
8. Compare against at least 5 local competitors
9. Save all outputs to output/audits/ with naming: {facility-slug}-{type}-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md
10. When estimating revenue leakage, show your math: X vacant units × $Y avg rent × Z months = $total lost
11. Recommendations should be prioritized: what to do first, second, third — not a random list
12. Always include a StowStack fit assessment — is this facility a good candidate for our services? Be honest.
