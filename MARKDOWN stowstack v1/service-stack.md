# StowStack — Service Stack

## S1: Free Facility Audit
- Entry point for all leads
- Research facility, market, competitors, digital presence
- Produce written audit report with diagnostic assessment
- Review on Zoom call — this is where trust is built

## S2: Meta Ad Campaign Management
- Campaign architecture by unit type and demand trigger
- Facebook + Instagram ad creation, targeting, optimization
- Lead form and landing page setup
- Retargeting campaigns
- Weekly optimization, monthly reporting

## S3: Landing Pages + Website Conversion
- Facility-specific landing pages for ad campaigns
- Website audit with specific conversion recommendations
- CTA optimization, offer structuring, urgency elements

## S4: Lead Routing + Follow-Up
- Speed-to-lead response systems
- Missed call recovery
- SMS/email nurture sequences
- Lead routing to facility staff

## S5: Operator Consulting
- Promotional strategy and pricing communication
- Concession strategy for lease-up
- Competitor positioning analysis
- Move-in conversion process review

## S6: Automation + SOPs
- Automation handbook
- Vendor recommendations (CRM, call tracking, etc.)
- Workflow guidance
- Standard operating procedures for lead handling
