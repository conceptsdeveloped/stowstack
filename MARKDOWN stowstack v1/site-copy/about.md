## Founder Section

### I built StowStack because I got tired of paying for reports full of clicks and zero move-ins.

My name is Blake Burkett. I have been in the self-storage business for 7 years. I have managed facilities. I have dealt with seasonal vacancy. I have watched occupancy dip heading into winter and scrambled to fill units before the numbers got ugly.

I know what it feels like to write a check to a marketing agency every month and have no idea whether it is doing anything. They send you a PDF with impressions, clicks, cost-per-click, and a line about how the campaign is "trending in the right direction." Meanwhile your vacancy has not moved.

I started building StowStack for my own facilities. I wanted three things:

1. **Ads that go to pages I control.** Not my homepage. Not a default rental page. A page I built for that specific ad, with the right headline, the right offer, and an embedded rental flow so the customer can reserve without leaving.

2. **Attribution that connects to move-ins.** Not clicks. Not form fills. I wanted to know which ad produced which reservation and which reservation became a move-in. Down to the dollar.

3. **Testing based on revenue.** I did not care which headline got more clicks. I wanted to know which headline produced more signed leases.

I built it. I tested it with my own money at my own facilities. It worked. Then operators I knew started asking how I was filling units so fast, and StowStack became a product.

## What StowStack Is Not

I want to be direct about what StowStack does and does not do. I have seen too many operators get sold something that was not what they thought.

**StowStack is not an SEO company.** We do not do organic search optimization. If you need SEO, there are good shops that do that. We can work alongside them.

**StowStack is not a SaaS dashboard.** We do not sell you software and tell you to figure it out. You get a team that builds, manages, and optimizes your campaigns.

**StowStack does not replace storEDGE.** We sit on top of it. storEDGE handles your reservations and payments. We handle everything that happens before the reservation — the ad, the page, the offer, the tracking.

**StowStack does not sell "marketing services."** We sell a closed-loop acquisition system. Every piece connects. The ad feeds the page. The page feeds the rental flow. The rental flow feeds the attribution. The attribution feeds the next round of optimization.

**StowStack does not optimize for clicks or impressions.** We optimize for reservations and move-ins. If an ad gets a thousand clicks and zero move-ins, we kill it. If an ad gets fifty clicks and eight move-ins, we scale it.

## The Competitive Landscape

### Where StowStack sits relative to the other options.

There are two other companies in the self-storage marketing space worth knowing about. Here is how they compare.

**SEO shops** build you a website and optimize for organic search. No paid traffic. No embedded rental flow. No attribution. Results take 3-6 months minimum. Great for the long game. Not great if you need units filled this quarter.

**Google-only platforms** run automated Google ads and send clicks to your default rental page. No custom landing pages. No Meta ads. No full-funnel attribution. Social media ads are a top-tier afterthought, not a core competency. They optimize for clicks, not leases.

**StowStack** creates demand through Meta ads, captures it through Google PPC, recaptures it through retargeting, sends every click to an ad-specific landing page with embedded storEDGE rental flow, and attributes every move-in back to the specific ad that produced it. Full funnel. Full visibility. Full control.
