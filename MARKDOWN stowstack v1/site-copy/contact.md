## FAQ Section

### Questions operators ask before they start.

**How fast will I see results?**
Most operators see leads within the first week of campaign launch. Move-ins typically start within 2-3 weeks. This is not SEO — you do not wait 6 months to find out if it is working.

**What do I need to get started?**
A storEDGE-powered facility (or willingness to adopt storEDGE), access to your facility details (unit mix, rates, current promotions), and a Meta ad account (we can set one up if you do not have one). We handle everything else.

**Do I need to change my PMS or rental software?**
No. StowStack sits on top of storEDGE. Your current systems stay the same. We embed the storEDGE rental flow into the landing pages we build. Nothing changes on your end operationally.

**What is the minimum ad spend?**
We recommend at least $1,000/mo in ad spend for a single facility. That is paid directly to Meta and Google — separate from StowStack's retainer. More spend means more data, faster optimization, and more move-ins.

**How is this different from hiring a regular marketing agency?**
Most agencies run your ads and send traffic to a page they do not control. They report clicks and impressions. They cannot tell you which ad produced which move-in. StowStack controls the ad, the page, the rental flow, and the attribution. It is a closed loop. Every dollar traces to a result.

**Do I have to sign a long-term contract?**
No. Month-to-month on the Demand Engine. The only commitment structure is the optional 6-month bundle discount on website builds. You can cancel anytime — but operators who stay 6+ months see their cost per move-in drop significantly as the A/B testing and attribution data compounds.

**What if I already have a website?**
You can start with the Demand Engine (Product A) only. We build ad-specific landing pages for your campaigns without replacing your existing site. If you later want a full site rebuild with embedded rental flow, we can add Product B.

**What if my facility is not on storEDGE?**
StowStack is currently built around storEDGE integration for the embedded rental flow. If you use a different PMS, reach out and we can discuss whether your system supports the kind of embedding we need. We are expanding integrations over time.

**Can you help with multiple facilities?**
Yes. The Portfolio tiers on both products are built for multi-facility operators. You get centralized management, cross-facility budget optimization, and volume discounts. The system scales — each facility gets its own campaigns, landing pages, and attribution data.
