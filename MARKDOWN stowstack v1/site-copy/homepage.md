Your agency sends you a report full of impressions and click-through rates. None of those numbers connect to a signed lease. You're told the campaign is "performing well" while your vacancy stays the same.

Here is what is actually happening.

## How It Works (Overview)

### From ad impression to signed lease. One system. Full visibility.

StowStack operates as a closed-loop system with four layers. Each feeds the next. Together they form a complete path from ad click to move-in — with full attribution at every step.

**Step 1: We create demand.**
Meta ads reach people before they search. Google PPC captures people already looking. Retargeting brings back the ones who left. Most operators only do one of these. We run all three.

**Step 2: Every ad gets its own landing page.**
Not your homepage. Not a generic rental page. A dedicated URL built for that specific campaign, with its own headline, its own offer, and its own tracking.

- `stowstacksite.com/climate-pawpaw-a` — for the climate-controlled search ad
- `stowstacksite.com/10x10-offer-b` — for the first-month-free Facebook ad
- `stowstacksite.com/finish-your-rental-c` — for the retargeting campaign

Different intent. Different pages. Different offers. That is how conversion rate goes up.

**Step 3: The rental flow is embedded in the page.**
storEDGE handles real-time availability, reservation processing, and payment. But the customer never leaves your branded page. They see your facility. Your branding. Your offer. And they rent — right there.

**Step 4: We tell you exactly which ad produced which move-in.**
Cost per reservation. Cost per move-in. Conversion rate by campaign, ad, audience, keyword, and creative. Not clicks. Not impressions. Revenue.
