# StowStack — ICP & Sales Process

## Ideal Customer
- Independent owners + small/mid-size operators (1-20 facilities)
- Buyer traits: skeptical, numbers-driven, anti-fluff, operationally busy
- They distrust generic marketers who don't understand storage
- They care about move-ins, occupancy, revenue — not vanity metrics
- Best fit: facilities with vacancy pain, weak digital presence, no/weak paid ads, poor conversion flow, poor follow-up, bad offer structure

## Sales Motion
1. Free facility audit (captured via website form or LinkedIn outreach)
2. Audit report generated (research + AI + Blake review)
3. Zoom call to walk through audit findings (30 min)
4. Proposal: pilot on best-candidate facility
5. Close → onboarding → campaign live in 48-72 hours
6. Retainer + ongoing optimization

## Sales Logic
- Customer buys filled units, not "Meta ads"
- Audit must separate: market problem, marketing problem, product problem, conversion/follow-up problem
- Be brutally honest about where ads can help and where they can't
- The audit itself is the best sales tool — honest, specific, credible

## Pricing
- Starter: $1,200/mo (single location, 2 ad sets, basic reporting)
- Growth: $2,200/mo (full-funnel, retargeting, conversion strategy, bi-weekly calls) — most popular
- Multi-Site: Custom (3+ locations, centralized reporting, dedicated management)
- Ad spend is separate, paid directly to Meta. Minimum recommended: $1,000/mo
