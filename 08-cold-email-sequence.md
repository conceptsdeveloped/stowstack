---

No pressure, but if you're interested in how we do it (and what we're actually seeing cost per move-in), I can send over a free facility audit. Takes 15 min, no strings.

## Sequence Notes

- **Timing:** Send Day 1, 3, 5, 7, 10 (Monday–Wednesday for Day 1 cold outreach)
- **List criteria:** Self-storage facilities, 1–20 units, active Google/Facebook ad spend signals (optional)
- **CTA throughout:** Free facility audit, 15 minutes
- **Response management:** Anyone who replies gets moved to a Sales/Blake calendar for a quick call
- **Unsubscribe:** Include standard unsubscribe language; respect opt-outs immediately
- **From:** Blake Burkett, StowStack (blake@stowstack.co or equivalent)

---

## Why This Sequence Works

1. **Email 1** breaks the pattern of vendor spam by establishing Blake as a fellow operator
2. **Email 2** agitates the core pain (attribution blindness)
3. **Email 3** provides social proof ($41 move-in cost, 71%→84% occupancy)
4. **Email 4** differentiates vs. agencies (not taking over ads, just enabling data)
5. **Email 5** is the permission-based final touch (low pressure, clear opt-out)

Each email stays under 150 words, uses short sentences, and has a clear next step (facility audit). Tone remains conversational and operator-to-operator throughout.
