## Notes on StowStack Positioning

1. **Ad-Specific Landing Pages:** Many keywords involve landing page optimization. Consistently position StowStack's ability to create ad-specific pages as the competitive edge.

2. **Full-Funnel Attribution:** Keywords around "ROI," "analytics," and "conversion" should highlight StowStack's proprietary full-funnel tracking as the missing piece competitors can't offer.

3. **Operator-Built Advantage:** Keywords in vendor comparison space ("marketing company," "advertising agency") should emphasize StowStack as operator-led, not just another agency.

4. **Meta Expertise:** Self-storage operators respond well to Meta ads. Content around Facebook/Instagram ads should showcase real case studies and channel expertise.

5. **Revenue-Based A/B Testing:** Keywords around testing and optimization should introduce revenue-based A/B testing as a StowStack differentiator versus vanity metrics.

## SEO Quick Wins

- **Keyword clustering:** Group related keywords into pillar posts + related blog articles (e.g., pillar on "occupancy," clusters on landing pages, analytics, budgeting).
- **Internal linking strategy:** Link related blog posts to each other and to product pages (e.g., CRO post → landing page builder; attribution post → dashboard).
- **FAQ sections:** Incorporate questions from themes into blog posts to capture voice search and People Also Ask queries.
- **Case studies:** Tie blog content to real StowStack client case studies (e.g., "X% occupancy increase in 30 days").
