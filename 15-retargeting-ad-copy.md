## Notes for Implementation

- **Audience:** Website visitors to stowstack.co who didn't convert (no call booked, no audit requested) in last 30–90 days.
- **Ad Format:** Single image (carousel optional) with headline + primary text + CTA.
- **Image Suggestion:** Clean photo of a full self-storage facility OR dashboard screenshot showing occupancy metrics.
- **Landing Page Target:** Audit request form (low friction, high intent).
- **Test Window:** Run all 8 for 2–3 weeks minimum; measure CTR, form submissions, cost per audit request.
- **Voice:** Operator-to-operator, specific numbers, no fluff.
