## FAQ

### Why is the build $3,000–5,000? Aren't there cheaper tools?

Yes. Wix, Squarespace, and page builders are $15–50/month. They're also generic. A self-storage operator's site needs to:
- Load fast (Core Web Vitals matter for Google Local Services)
- Embed a reservation form that feeds into your CRM
- Support A/B testing without needing a developer
- Work flawlessly on mobile (60%+ of your traffic)

I built the conversion layer specifically for storage operators. The build fee covers design, content, storEDGE embed integration, and setup. After that, hosting and maintenance are $199–299/month.

### What if I don't have $1,500/month in ad budget right now?

Start with **Launch** ($750/month) and $1,000/month in ad spend. Meta-only is still powerful. Test for 90 days, look at your cost per move-in, and decide if you want to add Google.

Most operators upgrade to Growth within 60 days because the results are obvious.

### What if it doesn't work?

Stop. You're month-to-month. No contract, no exit fees.

But here's what usually happens: After 30 days, you'll know your cost per move-in. If it's below $50, you scale up. If it's above $100, we optimize. Either way, you have data.

The only way it "doesn't work" is if your facility is genuinely uncompetitive (wrong price point, bad reviews, poor location). I can tell you that in the first month. If that's the case, StowStack isn't the answer — you need something else.

### How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

Agencies take a % of ad spend, usually 15–20%. They want you spending $5,000+/month on ads so their fee is worth it. They're incentivized to spend more, not smarter.

I take a flat fee. Your ad budget is your choice. I don't care if you spend $1,000 or $10,000/month — I make the same. That means I'm incentivized to get you results at any budget level.

Also: You own the site. You own the landing pages. You own the data. I'm not locking you into a platform or holding your leads hostage.

### Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Yes. The media fee is month-to-month. If you want to pause for a month, pause. If you want to cancel the conversion layer and keep your site, you can (though that's rare).

The only commitment is the optional 6-month bundle for the build discount. After 6 months, everything is month-to-month.

### What if I already have a website?

If it's solid (fast, mobile-friendly, good conversion flow), we can work with it. We'll integrate storEDGE and set up the landing pages. That's a smaller engagement.

If it's not, we rebuild. An old Wix site or a barely-used WordPress blog costs you move-ins.
