## 4. COMMUNICATION & EXPECTATIONS

### Team Roles & Responsibilities
- **Blake Burkett (Founder/Strategy):** Campaign strategy, creative direction, client relationship, performance reviews, optimization decisions
- **Anna Almeida (Onboarding/Ops):** Project coordination, access collection, info gathering, deliverable timeline, day-to-day status checks, documentation
- **Design/Development Team:** Landing page build, Ad creative, storEDGE embed integration, tracking setup

### Communication Cadence

**During Onboarding (Weeks 1-4)**
- Daily Slack/email updates to client on progress (brief, one-line status)
- Scheduled check-in calls: Day 1 (kickoff), Day 5 (design review), Day 10 (pre-launch), Day 21 (performance review)
- Ad hoc response to client questions: **24-hour target**

**Post-Launch (Ongoing)**
- Weekly performance reports (Mondays) emailed to client
- Bi-weekly optimization calls (first Monday and Thursday of each 2-week cycle)
- Monthly comprehensive performance review and strategy call
- Ad hoc alerts for any critical issues (spend anomalies, tracking errors, policy violations)
