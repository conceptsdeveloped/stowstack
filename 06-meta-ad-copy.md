## Notes on Deployment

- **Audience:** Self-storage facility owners/operators with 1-20 units, US-based, Facebook/Instagram users
- **Placement:** Feed ads (Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network)
- **CTA button:** "Learn More" or "See Demo" (route to demo dashboard or onboarding wizard)
- **Landing destination:** `stowstack.co/demo` or `/onboarding` (depends on funnel stage)
- **A/B test strategy:** Rotate these across 3-4 ad sets over 2 weeks, measure on landing page visits → demo requests → onboarding starts
